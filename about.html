About Mai

Mai is a rising Honors senior at Temple University studying Strategic Communication. Mai took Python, Java, and Discrete Maths in college to challenge herself and these courses change her life. Now, she is learning tech skills. Mai interned at VentureOut, a tech startup accelarator in Manhattan that raises over $1B as of Summer 2018. Before VentureOut, she has interned for a national non-profit & research organization Hepatitis B Foundation. She represented Pennsylvania two years in a row (2017 & 2018) to persuade Senator Bob Casey and Congressman Brian Fitzpatrick in person to raise research funding on the deadly disease Hepatitis B to $70 million.
